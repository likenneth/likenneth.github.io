Kenneth Li

I am a fourth-year PhD student at Harvard, advised by Fernanda Viégas, Hanspeter Pfister, and Martin Wattenberg. I am supported by Kempner Institute Graduate Fellowship. I hold a graduate-student Superalignment Fast Grant from OpenAI. I interned at MSR Asia and Meta AI.

I aim at understanding the inner workings of large language models and, based on these findings, control them for improved human-AI collaboration. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to discuss!
